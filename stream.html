Livestream

Thanks to the enthusiasum for DHGetDown, we have arranged to livestream several of the sessions in order to make them available to folks who cannot join us in person. The keynote panel and the final session will be livestreamed; these sessions will also be recorded and made available through the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa institutional repository.

To participate, please tune into our YouTube channel at the times below.

  • Keynote Panel: 9:00am HST – 10:20am HST
  • Closing Session: 3:00pm HST – 4:00pm HST

If you have a YouTube account, you can ask questions using the chat feature on livestream. If you prefer not to create an account, you can also ask questions via the collaborative note-taking document. We regret that we can only provide very limited technical support for the livestream.

Click here to access the livestream.

Collaborative Notes

Virtual and in-person participants are encouraged to contribute to our collaborative note-taking efforts. We will be taking notes from the event and fielding questions via a public Google Doc. Simply access the document, and follow the instructions to add your comments.

Notes will be archived in the institutional respository. You have the option of contributing anonymously if you are not signed in to a Google account (including your UH Google account). Please be mindful of the Code of Conduct.
